# Trade Review Framework

Use this checklist to review crypto futures trades.

## Core Principle

The review is about process quality, not only profit or loss. A profitable trade can be poor execution; a losing trade can be valid if the setup and risk were handled correctly.

## Review Checklist

### 1. Market Environment

- Did BTC/ETH support the trade direction?
- Was the market in startup, fermentation, divergence, repair, or retreat?
- Was this a strong leader/core pair, mid-cap core, catch-up pair, or follower?

### 2. Entry Quality

- Was there a clear setup: breakout, pullback confirmation, liquidation reversal, or breakdown?
- Was the entry confirmed by the watched timeframe?
- Was volume expanding on attack or shrinking on pullback?
- Did the user chase after the move was already extended?

### 3. Protection Line

- Was the protection line defined before entry?
- Was it placed at a structural invalidation level rather than an emotional number?
- Did price break the protection line?
- If broken, did the user reduce/exit instead of hoping?

### 4. Holding Decision

For long trades, continued holding requires:

- price above protection line
- no high-volume stagnation
- healthy pullback behavior
- market environment not clearly deteriorating
- funding/OI not dangerously crowded

For short trades, continued holding requires:

- price below rebound pressure/protection line
- weak rebound behavior
- no obvious reversal volume
- funding not extremely negative

### 5. Add / Reduce / Exit

- Adding is allowed only when the original logic has been further validated.
- Do not add to losing futures positions by default.
- Reduce when high-position volume-price stagnation, large upper wick, OI crowding, or funding extremes appear.
- Exit when protection line or original logic breaks.

## Process Score

Use this optional 0-100 process score:

- 20 market environment checked
- 20 entry had confirmation
- 20 protection line defined and respected
- 15 position size matched stop distance
- 15 add/reduce/exit followed rules
- 10 review notes produce a concrete next rule

Scores:

- 80+: good process, refine execution
- 60-79: usable idea, execution needs tightening
- 40-59: weak process, reduce size next time
- <40: do not repeat without a written plan

## Output Rule

End every review with one or two concrete rules that can be used before the next trade.
